Nashik Youth Model UN

Pioneering Student-Led Diplomatic Education Since 2024

Pioneering Non-Institutional MUN Platform

The Nashik Youth Model United Nations (NYMUN) Society is a pioneering, student-led organization in Nashik, making waves as the city's first non-institutional Model United Nations group. Our mission is to empower young minds by providing a space where they can actively engage in discussions on pressing global issues, all while honing critical skills in diplomacy, communication, and negotiation.

Led by a team of experienced college students, many of whom have organized large-scale events like TED Talks and university festivals, NYMUN offers a unique platform for skill-building that extends beyond the traditional MUN experience.

Delegate Development Program

Global Issue Analysis

Through our simulations, where delegates mimic the discussions and decisions made at the United Nations, participants explore international relations in depth, building confidence and refining public speaking abilities.

Diplomatic Communication

We emphasize clear and persuasive communication, strengthening delegates' ability to express and defend positions while developing critical thinking and response skills under pressure.

Organizing Committee Development

Event Management Mastery

OCs develop leadership, teamwork, and project management skills through conference planning, logistics coordination, and stakeholder communication.

Professional Skill Building

Members gain real-world experience in problem-solving, time management, and decision-making - essential traits for business success.

Global Leadership Network

NYMUN members participate in international conferences and youth parliaments, expanding professional networks while developing a nuanced understanding of global challenges.
